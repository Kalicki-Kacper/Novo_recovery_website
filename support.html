Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Novo Recovery?

Getting started is easy! Download the app from the App Store, launch it, and follow the onboarding process. You'll be guided through setting up your recovery tracking preferences and customizing the app to fit your needs.

Is my data secure and private?

Absolutely! Your privacy is our top priority. All your personal data is stored exclusively on your device and never leaves it. We don't have access to your recovery data, journal entries, or any personal information you track. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.

Can I sync my data across multiple devices?

Currently, Novo Recovery stores all data locally on your device to ensure maximum privacy. This means your data doesn't automatically sync across devices. We recommend using the app primarily on one device to maintain your recovery records.

What happens to my data if I uninstall the app?

Because all data is stored locally on your device, uninstalling Novo Recovery will permanently delete all your data. Before uninstalling, we recommend taking screenshots or backing up any important information you want to keep.

How do I backup my data?

Since data is stored locally, the best way to backup your information is through your device's standard backup process (iCloud or iTunes backup). Additionally, you can take screenshots of important entries or manually export data you want to preserve.

How do I upgrade to the lifetime version?

You can upgrade to the lifetime version directly within the app. Look for the "Upgrade" or "Premium" option in the app settings or menu. All purchases are processed securely through Apple's App Store. Please note that prices are subject to change and may increase at any time.

I purchased the app but it's not unlocking the premium features. What should I do?

First, try closing and reopening the app. If that doesn't work, go to your device's Settings > [Your Name] > Media & Purchases > View Account, and check your purchase history. If you still have issues, please contact us at kumkwat_splata.9n@icloud.com with your purchase receipt.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

If you've purchased the lifetime version, you can restore your purchase on a new device. Look for the "Restore Purchase" option in the app settings. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

Can I get a refund?

All purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. Refund requests must be made directly to Apple. You can request a refund by going to reportaproblem.apple.com and following the instructions. Apple's refund policy applies to all purchases.

The app is crashing or not working properly. What should I do?

We're sorry you're experiencing issues! Please try the following steps:

  • Force close the app and reopen it
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure you have the latest version of the app installed
  • Make sure your iOS is up to date

If the problem persists, please contact us at kumkwat_splata.9n@icloud.com with details about the issue, including your device model and iOS version.

How do I disable notifications?

You can control notifications in two ways:

  • Within the app: Check the app's settings menu for notification preferences
  • Device settings: Go to Settings > Notifications > Novo Recovery and adjust your notification preferences

Can I request a new feature?

Absolutely! We love hearing feature requests from our users. Please send your ideas to kumkwat_splata.9n@icloud.com. While we can't implement every request, we carefully consider all feedback when planning future updates.

Is Novo Recovery a medical app?

No, Novo Recovery is a wellness tracking app and is not intended for medical use. It should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with qualified healthcare providers regarding any medical conditions or concerns. For more information, please see our Terms of Use.

Tips for Getting the Most Out of Novo Recovery

Set Up Daily Reminders

Enable notifications to remind you to track your recovery progress consistently. Regular tracking helps you see patterns and progress over time.

Be Consistent

The more consistently you track your recovery, the more valuable insights you'll gain. Try to update your progress at the same time each day.

Use Journal Features

Take advantage of note-taking and journaling features to record your thoughts, feelings, and experiences. This can help you identify triggers and celebrate victories.

Review Your Progress Regularly

Take time each week to review your progress. Seeing how far you've come can be incredibly motivating and help you stay committed to your recovery journey.

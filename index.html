Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Novo Recovery?

Download the app from the App Store, launch it, and follow the onboarding process to set up your recovery plan.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. All data is stored locally on your device and never leaves it. Please see our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I sync across multiple devices?

Currently, Novo Recovery stores all data locally on your device to ensure maximum privacy. Your data doesn't automatically sync across devices.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 29, 2025

Your Privacy is Our Priority

Novo Recovery is designed with privacy at its core. All your personal data stays on your device, and we have no access to it.

What We Don't Collect

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect your name, email, or any identifying information
  • No Health Data: Your recovery metrics and wellness information remain exclusively on your device
  • No Location Data: We do not collect or track your location

What We Do Collect

We collect anonymous, aggregated analytics through Apple's App Store Connect to improve app performance. This data cannot identify individual users.

You can opt out of analytics: Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements > Disable "Share iPhone Analytics"

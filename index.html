Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Novo Recovery?

Download the app from the App Store, create an account, and follow the onboarding process to set up your recovery plan.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. All data is encrypted and stored securely. Please see our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I sync across multiple devices?

Yes, your recovery data syncs automatically across all your devices when you're signed in with the same account.
